FAQ

Are your products authentic?
Yes. We source from official distributors only, and our QC flow includes seal/box checks and barcode validation.
What if my figure arrives damaged?
Contact us within 7 days with photos. We’ll assist with replacement or parts coordination with the distributor.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes. We use tracked methods and extra padding/wrap for long routes.
